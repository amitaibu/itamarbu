About

Center content

Born in Haifa, Israel in 1974. Graduated the Jerusalem Bezalel academy of art & design – Industrial design department. Since his graduation in 2002 has been working on a wide range of projects with a large number of international companies. Working in his studio, his designs vary from Plastic injected house hold items & electrical appliances to high-end Furniture and light objects. Since 2006 Itamar's designs have been supported time and again by VIA France. His designs have been endorsed by companies such as- VIA France, Roche Bobois, Metalarte, Ligne Roset & Jofebar.
